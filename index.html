HOW TO PLAY

THE GOAL

Every level hides a secret word — the code. Explore the scene: look, listen, count, experiment. When you think you have it, type it in the code box at the bottom of the screen and press EXECUTE (or hit Enter). A wrong code just flashes red — guessing costs nothing, but the answer is always earned.

THE CONTROLS

EXECUTE

Submits the code you typed in the box next to it.

⟳

Restarts the current level from scratch.

INFO

A short, cryptic hint for the current level. It sets the mood and points a direction — it never spells out the answer.

🔊

Game volume. Drag the slider, or click the icon to mute and unmute.

New Game

Wipes all progress and starts over from Level 1.

Options

Music and sound-effect volume settings.

THE LEVELS PANEL

🔒

THE BADGES

Open INFO and you may find badges under the title. They tell you what kind of attention the level wants:

🔊SOUND

Audio matters here. Turn the volume up and listen carefully — something is said only in sound.

🔧TOOL

The level speaks a language you may need to look up: an alphabet, a cipher, a notation. Decoding it is the puzzle.

ONE LAST THING

Everything you need is inside the room. Nothing is random — if something feels slightly wrong, it probably is. That is the way in.
